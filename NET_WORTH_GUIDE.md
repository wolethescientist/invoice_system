# Net Worth Tracking - Complete Guide

## Overview

The Net Worth Tracking feature provides comprehensive tools to monitor your financial health by tracking assets, liabilities, and calculating your net worth over time. It includes projections, trend analysis, and alerts for significant changes.

## Features

### 1. Asset Management
- Track multiple asset types (checking, savings, investments, retirement, real estate, vehicles, crypto, etc.)
- Mark assets as liquid or illiquid
- Store institution details and account information
- Historical value tracking with snapshots
- Active/inactive status for flexible management

### 2. Liability Management
- Track various debt types (credit cards, loans, mortgages, etc.)
- Record interest rates and minimum payments
- Monitor total debt obligations
- Historical balance tracking
- Calculate total monthly debt payments

### 3. Net Worth Calculation
- Automatic calculation: Assets - Liabilities = Net Worth
- Real-time updates when assets or liabilities change
- Liquid assets tracking for emergency fund analysis
- Breakdown by asset and liability types

### 4. Historical Tracking
- Create snapshots to track net worth over time
- View trends for 6, 12, 24, or 36 months
- Compare current vs. historical values
- Track 30-day, 90-day, and 1-year changes

### 5. Projections
- Project future net worth based on:
  - Active financial goals and contributions
  - Scheduled debt payments
  - Current trends
- Adjustable projection periods (6-60 months)
- View assumptions and methodology

### 6. Alerts & Notifications
- Significant increase alerts (>10% change)
- Significant decrease warnings (>10% change)
- Negative net worth alerts
- Low liquidity warnings
- Customizable alert thresholds

### 7. Visual Analytics
- Net worth trend charts
- Asset breakdown pie charts
- Liability breakdown pie charts
- Projection comparison charts
- Interactive data visualization

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Add Your Assets

1. Navigate to **Net Worth** ‚Üí **Manage Assets**
2. Click **Add Asset**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Name (e.g., "Chase Checking")
   - Asset Type
   - Current Value
   - Institution (optional)
   - Account Number (last 4 digits, optional)
   - Mark as liquid if easily convertible to cash
4. Click **Create Asset**

**Asset Types:**
- **Cash**: Physical cash on hand
- **Checking**: Checking accounts
- **Savings**: Savings accounts
- **Investment**: Brokerage accounts, stocks, bonds
- **Retirement**: 401(k), IRA, pension accounts
- **Real Estate**: Property, land
- **Vehicle**: Cars, boats, motorcycles
- **Crypto**: Cryptocurrency holdings
- **Other**: Any other assets

### Step 2: Add Your Liabilities

1. Navigate to **Net Worth** ‚Üí **Manage Liabilities**
2. Click **Add Liability**
3. Fill in the details:
   - Name (e.g., "Chase Credit Card")
   - Liability Type
   - Current Balance
   - Interest Rate
   - Minimum Monthly Payment
   - Institution (optional)
4. Click **Create Liability**

**Liability Types:**
- **Credit Card**: Credit card debt
- **Student Loan**: Education loans
- **Mortgage**: Home loans
- **Auto Loan**: Vehicle financing
- **Personal Loan**: Personal loans
- **Medical Debt**: Medical bills
- **Other**: Any other debts

### Step 3: Create Your First Snapshot

1. Go to the main **Net Worth** page
2. Click **Create Snapshot**
3. This captures your current net worth for historical tracking

**Best Practice:** Create snapshots regularly (weekly or monthly) to track trends accurately.

### Step 4: Review Your Net Worth

The dashboard displays:
- **Current Net Worth**: Total assets minus total liabilities
- **Total Assets**: Sum of all active assets
- **Total Liabilities**: Sum of all active debts
- **Liquid Assets**: Easily accessible funds
- **Change Metrics**: 30-day, 90-day, and 1-year changes

## Using the Features

### Viewing Trends

1. On the Net Worth page, view the **Net Worth Trends** chart
2. Select time range: 6, 12, 24, or 36 months
3. The chart shows:
   - Net worth over time (green line)
   - Total assets (blue line)
   - Total liabilities (red line)

### Asset & Liability Breakdown

The breakdown charts show:
- Distribution by type
- Percentage of total
- Count of items in each category
- For liabilities: total interest rates and minimum payments

### Viewing Projections

1. Click **View Projection** on the Net Worth page
2. Select projection period (6-60 months)
3. Review:
   - Projected net worth
   - Projected assets and liabilities
   - Expected change
   - Assumptions used in calculation

**Projection Methodology:**
- Based on active financial goals and monthly contributions
- Considers scheduled debt payments
- Assumes consistent contribution and payment patterns
- Does not account for market fluctuations or unexpected changes

### Managing Alerts

Alerts appear at the top of the Net Worth page when:
- Net worth changes significantly (>10%)
- Net worth becomes negative
- Liquid assets are low relative to liabilities

### Updating Asset Values

1. Go to **Manage Assets**
2. Click **Edit** on the asset
3. Update the **Current Value**
4. Click **Save**

A snapshot is automatically created when you update values, allowing you to track changes over time.

### Updating Liability Balances

1. Go to **Manage Liabilities**
2. Click **Edit** on the liability
3. Update the **Current Balance**
4. Update **Interest Rate** or **Minimum Payment** if changed
5. Click **Save**

### Deactivating Assets or Liabilities

Instead of deleting, you can deactivate items:
1. Edit the asset or liability
2. Uncheck **Active**
3. Click **Save**

Inactive items are excluded from net worth calculations but preserved for historical records.

## Best Practices

### Regular Updates
- Update asset values monthly (or when statements arrive)
- Update liability balances after making payments
- Create snapshots consistently (same day each month)

### Accurate Tracking
- Use current market values for investments and real estate
- Include all assets, even small ones
- Don't forget to include all debts
- Update interest rates when they change

### Goal Setting
- Set financial goals in the Financial Roadmap feature
- Link goals to net worth tracking for accurate projections
- Review projections quarterly
- Adjust goals based on actual progress

### Emergency Fund
- Monitor liquid assets closely
- Aim for 3-6 months of expenses in liquid assets
- Pay attention to liquidity warnings
- Keep some assets easily accessible

### Debt Management
- Track all minimum payments
- Monitor total monthly debt obligations
- Use projections to plan debt payoff
- Prioritize high-interest debt

## Troubleshooting

### Net Worth Seems Incorrect
- Verify all assets are marked as active
- Check that all liabilities are included
- Ensure values are current
- Create a new snapshot to refresh calculations

### Projections Don't Match Expectations
- Review projection assumptions
- Check active financial goals
- Verify debt payment amounts
- Remember projections are estimates

### Missing Historical Data
- Snapshots must be created manually
- Past data cannot be recreated
- Start creating regular snapshots now
- Historical trends will build over time

### Charts Not Displaying
- Ensure you have created at least one snapshot
- Check that you have assets or liabilities
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache if needed

## API Endpoints

For developers integrating with the API:

### Assets
- `GET /api/net-worth/assets` - List all assets
- `POST /api/net-worth/assets` - Create asset
- `GET /api/net-worth/assets/{id}` - Get asset details
- `PUT /api/net-worth/assets/{id}` - Update asset
- `DELETE /api/net-worth/assets/{id}` - Delete asset

### Liabilities
- `GET /api/net-worth/liabilities` - List all liabilities
- `POST /api/net-worth/liabilities` - Create liability
- `GET /api/net-worth/liabilities/{id}` - Get liability details
- `PUT /api/net-worth/liabilities/{id}` - Update liability
- `DELETE /api/net-worth/liabilities/{id}` - Delete liability

### Analytics
- `GET /api/net-worth/summary` - Get net worth summary
- `GET /api/net-worth/trends?months=12` - Get trend data
- `GET /api/net-worth/breakdown/assets` - Get asset breakdown
- `GET /api/net-worth/breakdown/liabilities` - Get liability breakdown
- `GET /api/net-worth/projection?months=12` - Get projection
- `GET /api/net-worth/alerts` - Get alerts
- `POST /api/net-worth/snapshots` - Create snapshot

## Security & Privacy

- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Only last 4 digits of account numbers are stored
- Data is user-specific and not shared
- Regular backups protect your data
- You can export your data at any time
